Project Overview

RA Logistics is proud to partner with Zomato Hyperpure, India's leading B2B supply platform for restaurants. Our collaboration focuses on developing state-of-the-art warehousing and distribution facilities that support Hyperpure's mission to deliver high-quality, fresh ingredients to restaurants across the country.

These specialized facilities are designed with temperature-controlled zones, advanced inventory management systems, and efficient logistics workflows to ensure the freshness and quality of produce, dairy, and other perishable items. Our infrastructure enables Hyperpure to maintain their commitment to food safety standards while optimizing their supply chain operations.

Through this partnership, we're helping Zomato Hyperpure scale their operations efficiently while maintaining the quality and reliability that restaurants depend on for their daily operations.
