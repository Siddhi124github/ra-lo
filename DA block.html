The DA Block project is an ongoing development by RA Logistics, aimed at delivering a modern and efficient warehousing facility. Designed with a focus on operational fluidity and scalability, this facility reflects our forward-thinking approach to logistics infrastructure.

Located in Bhiwandi, the site benefits from excellent connectivity, making it a strategic hub for streamlined distribution and supply chain operations.
