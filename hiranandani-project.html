Hiranandani Industrial Park

Bhiwandi, Maharashtra

Project Overview

The Hiranandani Industrial Park represents RA Logistics' commitment to excellence in warehouse development. This state-of-the-art facility was completed in 2023 and has since become a benchmark for modern industrial warehousing in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region.

Strategically located in Bhiwandi, the warehouse offers unparalleled connectivity to major highways and ports, making it an ideal choice for e-commerce, manufacturing, and distribution companies looking to optimize their supply chain operations.
