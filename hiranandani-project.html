Project Overview

The Hiranandani Industrial Park reflects RA Logistics' ongoing commitment to excellence in warehouse development. This state-of-the-art facility is currently under development and is poised to set new benchmarks for modern industrial warehousing in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region.
